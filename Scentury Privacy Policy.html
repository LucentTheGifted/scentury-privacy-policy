Scentury App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Scentury App ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and discloses information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information in connection with the services we provide through the App.

a. Information You Provide Directly:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and a password. You may also provide a nickname for your user profile.
  • User-Generated Content: Information you input related to your perfume collections, such as:
    • Perfumes you mark as "Owned."
    • Perfumes you add to your "Wishlist."
    • Custom "Wardrobe Categories" and the perfumes assigned to them.
  • Communication Data: If you contact us for support or inquiries (e.g., via the "Support" screen), we may collect the content of your communications.

b. Information Collected Automatically (via Third-Party Services):

When you use our App, certain information may be automatically collected by third-party services integrated into the App. This data is collected to provide services, improve app functionality, display ads, and for analytics.

  • Usage and Analytics Data (via Google Analytics for Firebase and Firebase Crashlytics):
    • Information about how you interact with the App (e.g., screens visited, features used, button clicks, time spent in the app).
    • Device information (e.g., device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers).
    • Crash logs and performance data.
    • Geographic location (derived from IP address, not precise GPS).
  • Advertising Data (via Google Mobile Ads/AdMob):
    • Ad identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID, IDFA for iOS).
    • IP address.
    • Information about ad impressions, clicks, and conversions.
    • Device type, operating system.
  • Authentication Data (via Firebase Authentication):
    • User IDs generated by Firebase to manage your account and link your data.
    • Timestamps of authentication events.
  • Local Storage Data (via SharedPreferences):
    • We use shared_preferences to locally store some app settings (e.g., dark mode preference, language selection) and user-specific data like your owned perfumes, wishlist, and wardrobe categories to ensure a smooth user experience. This data is stored on your device and may or may not be synced to cloud services like Firestore depending on the specific implementation.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the App: To operate, maintain, and provide all features of the App, including managing user accounts, storing your perfume collections, and delivering perfume recommendations.
  • To Personalize Your Experience: To tailor the app's content and features to your preferences, such as displaying your saved perfumes and applying your chosen settings (e.g., dark mode, language).
  • To Improve and Develop the App: To understand how users interact with the App, identify areas for improvement, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • To Display Advertisements: To show you relevant ads through Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to support the free operation of the App.
  • To Ensure Security: To protect the security and integrity of the App and our services, including preventing fraud and abuse.
  • To Communicate with You: To send you password reset emails (if requested) or respond to your support inquiries.
  • For Analytics and Research: To perform internal analytics and research to better understand our user base and improve our offerings.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: We may share information if you give us explicit permission to do so.
  • With Third-Party Service Providers: We use third-party services that process data on our behalf for the purposes described above. These include:
    • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Analytics, Crashlytics): For user authentication, storing user profiles and collection data, app usage analytics, and crash reporting. Google's Privacy Policy applies to their services.
    • Google Mobile Ads (AdMob): For serving advertisements. Google's Privacy Policy applies to their ad services.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order or government agency).
  • To Enforce Our Policies: To enforce our terms and conditions and protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property, and that of our users or the public.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with, or during negotiations of, any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business by another company.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services, or as required by law. If you delete your account, we will delete your associated personal data, though some aggregated or anonymized data derived from your usage may be retained for analytical purposes.

5. Your Choices and Rights

  • Account Information: You can access and update your nickname through the "My Profile" screen in the App. You can request to delete your account by contacting us (see Section 9).
  • Opt-Out of Personalized Ads: You may be able to limit personalized ads through your device settings (e.g., by resetting your advertising ID) or by using "AdChoices" tools provided by ad networks.
  • Device Permissions: You can enable or disable location services, push notifications, and other device permissions through your device settings.

6. Security of Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected through the App from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without verifiable parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
